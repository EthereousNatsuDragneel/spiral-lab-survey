SPIRAL Lab Audio Classification Survey

This survey is conducted by the University of British Columbia (UBC) and hosted on the Prolific platform. You are welcome to participate without a Prolific account, but only Prolific users are eligible for payment.

The survey contains approximately 4000 audio files. You are free to classify as many files as you like, with no obligation to complete them all.

Each audio file will be accompanied by 10 range sliders, each representing different sound attributes. Your task is to adjust these sliders to best describe the sound you hear.

  • Attribute 1: (Accidental ↔ Deliberate)
  • Attribute 2: (Aggressive ↔ Peaceful)
  • etc.

After completing each classification, you can either:

  • Select "Next Question" to continue with the survey, or
  • Select "Finish Survey" to exit and be redirected to Prolific.
